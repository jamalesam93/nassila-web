Nassila ships **Sanad** passage grounding against **local** GGUF models you download yourself. Multi-GB weights are **not** bundled in the installer.

<Callout variant="warning" title="AI-assisted">
Local models power **AI-assisted** passage checks. They do not replace L1/L2 registry verification or manual review before submission.
</Callout>

## Available models

| Artifact | Model ID | Base | Quant | VRAM (approx.) | Role |
|----------|----------|------|-------|----------------|------|
| Sanad default | `nassila-sanad-e4b` | Gemma 4 E4B | Q6_K ~8 GB | ~8 GB | Default tier in Settings |
| Sanad quality | `nassila-sanad-12b` | Gemma 4 12B | Q6_K | ~12 GB+ | Quality tier chip |

**Download (public Hugging Face):**

- [nassila-sanad-e4b](https://huggingface.co/QinEmPeRoR93/nassila-sanad-e4b)
- [nassila-sanad-12b](https://huggingface.co/QinEmPeRoR93/nassila-sanad-12b)

Typical filenames: `nassila-sanad-e4b-q6_k.gguf`, `nassila-sanad-12b-q6_k.gguf`.

## Tier behavior in the app

| Tier | When to use |
|------|-------------|
| **E4B** | Default; laptop-friendly; recommended starting point |
| **12B** | Higher-quality grounding when you have sufficient VRAM/RAM |

Switch tiers from the **Sanad bar** tier chip or **Settings → Passage grounding**.

## Runners

Sanad talks to any **OpenAI-compatible** `/v1/chat/completions` endpoint:

| Runner | Default base URL |
|--------|------------------|
| LM Studio | `http://localhost:1234` |
| Ollama | `http://localhost:11434` |
| vLLM | Your server URL |
| Custom | User-defined |

Setup walkthrough: [Sanad setup](/docs/sanad-setup).

## What Sanad receives at inference

- **Your passage** — text around the in-text citation (capped by engine limits)
- **Source excerpt** — registry abstract or OA chunk (up to engine excerpt cap)
- **Structured JSON** output — claims, verdicts, verbatim quotes when supported

Excerpt type in current product builds is primarily **abstract-first**; OA full text is used when manuscript source fetch succeeds.

## Distribution policy

- **Bring your own GGUF** or use Ollama pull from Hugging Face Hub
- No automatic download of model weights without your action
- Cloud API preset optional — you supply endpoint and credentials

## Future unified model

A merged **`nassila-agent-*`** multi-worker bundle is planned for later Ouroboros releases. Today, ship path uses **Sanad-only** GGUFs above.
