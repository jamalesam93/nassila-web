The **Manuscript** mode is Nassila's Ouroboros loop: one connected workflow from upload to audit to export — not seven disconnected tools.

*Verify your references. Ground your claims.*

## What the loop does

<Steps>
<Step title="Upload manuscript">
Paste text or import **DOCX** / **PDF**. Nassila extracts structure, in-text citations, and the **References** section.
</Step>
<Step title="Resolve cited sources">
For each cite, run **L1/L2** registry checks. Fetch registry abstracts, Europe PMC text, or Unpaywall open-access HTML where available.
</Step>
<Step title="Run audit">
When **Passage grounding** is enabled, **Sanad** (L3) compares your passage around each cite to the source excerpt and returns structured verdicts with quotes.
</Step>
<Step title="Review and export">
Read mismatch cards, grounding panels, and inline **Tasnif** / **Sharh** copy. Export bibliography or audit summary when ready.
</Step>
</Steps>

## Before you audit

<Callout variant="warning" title="Bibliography-first">
If your embedded References list is messy — wrong numbering, missing DOIs, bad plain-text parse — audit quality suffers. **Curate in Bibliography first**, then return to Manuscript. See [Bibliography bridge](/docs/bibliography-bridge).
</Callout>

## After an audit

The **cited-sources table** lists each in-text citation. Expand a row for detail:

| Panel | Meaning |
|-------|---------|
| **Your passage** | Text from your manuscript around the cite |
| **Source excerpt** | Chunk sent to Sanad (abstract or OA text) |
| **Claims + quotes** | Per-claim verdict; verbatim quotes when supported |
| **Source text** header | `Abstract only`, `Open-access full text`, or `Unavailable` |

**L1 / L2 / L3** chips summarize registry match, metadata alignment, and passage grounding.

### Coverage labels

- **Abstract only** — No OA full text; Sanad used the registry abstract.
- **Open-access full text** — Europe PMC or Unpaywall supplied HTML/JATS (may be chunk-selected).
- **Source text unavailable** — No abstract and no OA text; L3 is limited.

## Sanad bar

At the top of the manuscript view:

- **Toggle** — enable/disable grounding for this session
- **Tier chip** — **E4B** (default) or **12B** (quality)
- **Setup / Configure** — Sanad modal and Passage grounding settings

Setup: [Sanad setup](/docs/sanad-setup).

## Unpaywall email

Settings → **General → Manuscript source fetch** — one-time email for Unpaywall OA lookups. Stored locally only; **not** university paywall login.

| Ground | Sanad | Live — L3 when enabled |
| Risk | Tasnif | Live — inline in loop |
| Explain | Sharh | Partial — deterministic copy |
| Evidence | Shahid | Disabled — Tier 3+ |

Full glossary: [Workers](/docs/workers).

## What is not shipped yet

- Cited **PDF attach** in audits (Masdar)
- Automatic Maktab→Masdar→Sanad pipeline without manual steps
- Table/figure evidence (Shahid)

Honest stubs appear in the UI — no fake progress bars.
