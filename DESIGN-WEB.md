# Nassila website — design checklist

Extends [Nassila DESIGN.md](https://github.com/jamalesam93/Nassila/blob/main/docs/DESIGN.md) Impeccable bans to the public site.

## Ship

- IBM Plex Sans + Source Serif 4 + app teal palette (`lib/tokens.css`)
- Real product media (banner, app chrome frames) — no stock illustrations
- Asymmetric layouts; citation samples in serif
- Subtle motion; `prefers-reduced-motion` honored
- Bilingual EN/AR with native RTL
- Ouroboros diagram: **hero-scale** elliptic orbit; stage hues from Nassila semantics (teal, audit green, warn amber) — not decorative micro-graphics beside a duplicate list

## Reject before merge

- Purple/blue gradient heroes, glassmorphism, neon glows
- Inter/Arial/system-ui as the entire identity
- Identical icon + heading + blurb card grids
- Hero metrics (big number + small label)
- Uppercase tracked section eyebrows
- "Powered by AI" copy, sparkle icons, chat mockups
- Stock photos of researchers at laptops
- Default Starlight/Docusaurus/shadcn marketing templates
